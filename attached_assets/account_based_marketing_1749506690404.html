Precision-Targeted Marketing for High-Value Accounts

ELP Data's Account-Based Marketing (ABM) solutions help B2B organizations identify, engage, and convert their most valuable target accounts through highly personalized, multi-channel campaigns. Our comprehensive ABM approach combines premium data intelligence, strategic planning, and coordinated execution to create meaningful connections with key decision-makers at your ideal customer accounts.

Target Account Selection

Data-driven identification and prioritization of high-value accounts that align with your ideal customer profile and revenue potential.

Buying Committee Mapping

Comprehensive identification of all key stakeholders and decision-makers within target accounts, including roles, responsibilities, and relationships.

Personalized Content

Custom content development that addresses specific pain points, challenges, and objectives of each target account and buying role.

Multi-Channel Orchestration

Coordinated engagement across digital advertising, email, direct mail, events, and sales outreach for maximum impact and conversion.

Our Account-Based Marketing Approach

1

Strategic Planning

We begin by understanding your business objectives, ideal customer profile, and revenue goals. This foundation guides the development of a tailored ABM strategy that aligns marketing and sales efforts around high-value target accounts.

2

Account Selection & Research

Using our premium data intelligence and advanced analytics, we identify and prioritize target accounts based on fit, intent, and opportunity size. We then conduct deep research on each account to understand their business challenges, initiatives, and organizational structure.

3

Buying Committee Mapping

We map the complete buying committee within each target account, identifying key decision-makers, influencers, and gatekeepers. This includes detailed contact information, role insights, and relationship dynamics to enable personalized engagement.

4

Content & Messaging Development

Our team creates personalized content and messaging strategies for each target account and buying role, addressing their specific challenges, objectives, and industry context to maximize relevance and engagement.

5

Multi-Channel Campaign Execution

We implement coordinated, multi-channel campaigns that engage target accounts through digital advertising, email, direct mail, events, and sales outreach, creating multiple touchpoints throughout the buyer's journey.

6

Measurement & Optimization

We continuously monitor campaign performance using account-based metrics, providing detailed reporting on engagement, pipeline impact, and ROI. This data drives ongoing optimization and refinement of your ABM strategy.

Client Success Story

A B2B software company specializing in enterprise workflow solutions was struggling to penetrate large enterprise accounts with their traditional lead generation approach. After implementing our Account-Based Marketing solution:

  • Identified and prioritized 50 high-value target accounts based on fit, intent, and revenue potential
  • Mapped complete buying committees with an average of 14 stakeholders per account
  • Developed account-specific content addressing unique industry challenges and use cases
  • Implemented coordinated multi-channel campaigns across digital advertising, email, direct mail, and events
  • Results: 78% of target accounts engaged with multiple pieces of content, 42% took meetings with sales, and 28% converted to opportunities within 6 months
  • ROI: 386% return on ABM investment with 3 closed enterprise deals generating $1.2M in new revenue
